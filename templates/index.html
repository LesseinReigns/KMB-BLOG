ABOUT

Do you need ministers that help you connect to the heavenlies through spirit filled and heart felt worship?!! We are The Kingdom Mouthpiece and we raise altars of undiluted worship to the Most High God,we love and worship the Lord in spirit and in truth as he has commanded us. We are dedicated to bringing an heaven on earth experience by magnifying, adoring and worship the Lord God Almighty. Our vision is to equip the saints to acknowledge, worship and revere The Lord God Almighty and also to prepare for the end-time through psalms, hymns, spiritual ministrations, worshiping and rendering deep but melodious spirit sound. Our mission is to edify the body of Christ through love, worship and praise to our father. We are Hell Razers
